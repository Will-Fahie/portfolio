My experience

I am in no way an "expert" programmer. I first discovered programming in GCSE Computer Science, using Python. I did little bits here and there, making some small projects like a calculator, notepad, login system. It was only when I started A level Computer Science that my fascination and enjoyment in programming spiked. I learned the basics of Object-Oriented Programming through the A level course, however it was the work I did outside of the syllabus where I gained the most experience.

I completed two 30 to 40 hour online courses on Udemy (click here to view them), and played around with a few different languages. My experience/knowledge in programming is about 90% python-based. However, I have done some work with C++, Swift, HTML and CSS. I am always growing my knowledge, and intend to learn and work with languages other than Python going into the future.
