"A learn-it-all is better than a know-it-all"

Magdrive

In my research for my Extended Project Qualification (EPQ) on humans living self-sufficient on Mars (click here to view), I was fortunate enough to interview Thomas Clayson, the CTO of a rocket propulsion company called Magdrive. At the end of the interview, he offered me an internship over the summer of 2021 up at Magdrive. It was a fascinating experience, where I researched emerging propulsion companies and technologies, orbital manufacturing and asteroid mining!



Siemens Work Experience Programme

During the Easter holidays of April 2021, I completed the Siemens Virtual Work Experience programme. It gave me a fantastic insight into the engineering and technology industries, and really conveyed what it would be like to work for a big company such as Siemens.



The Orchards School of Cookery (video editing)

As explained in the video editing section, over the summer of the first national lockdown, I was contracted by the Oxford Events Company to compile and edit the footage for The Orchard's School of Cookery's first online cookery course. With a few dozen hours of footage to work through, it was tough, but I made sure to make the deadline! After the success of the first one, I was asked to edit their second course in 2021.
